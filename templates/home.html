About Our Project

Our Hospital Stay Length Prediction system uses advanced machine learning techniques to forecast how long a patient might need to stay in the hospital based on their medical condition and personal factors. This helps hospitals optimize bed allocation, plan staff schedules, and improve overall patient care.

The system leverages explainable AI to provide transparent insights into prediction factors, ensuring healthcare professionals understand the reasoning behind each forecast.

Explainable AI

Our system doesn't just predict - it explains why. Using SHAP and LIME techniques, we provide transparent insights into prediction factors that influence hospital stay duration.

Clinical Integration

Designed to integrate seamlessly with hospital information systems, providing real-time predictions at the point of care to support clinical decision-making.

Privacy Focused

All patient data is anonymized and encrypted, ensuring compliance with HIPAA and other healthcare regulations while maintaining data integrity.

SQLite3 Database Integration

Our application uses SQLite3 as the backend database to securely store:

  • User registration and authentication details
  • Patient medical information and prediction history
  • Medical precautions and recommendations
  • System configuration and settings

This lightweight, serverless database ensures reliability and performance while maintaining data security.
